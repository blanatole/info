**Authors:** Nguyen Hong Vu, Pham Ngoc Bao, Hoang Thi Minh Anh, Vu Phu Loc, Huynh Thi Cam Dung, Minh Y. Nguyen, Luu Van Nhat Hao, Thien Khai Tran  
**DOI:** [10.1007/978-3-032-21625-0_17](https://doi.org/10.1007/978-3-032-21625-0_17)  
**Book chapter:** Computational Intelligence in Engineering Science, ICCIES 2026, Communications in Computer and Information Science, Volume 2943, pages 233-247  
**First online:** 5 April 2026

This paper proposes the Content-Style Invariant Detector (CSID), a representation-level approach for robust fake news detection under style-transfer attacks. The method uses semantic anchoring and contrastive representation stabilization to preserve semantic consistency across stylistic variations.
