**Authors:** Thanh-Long Nguyen, Nguyen Khanh Vy, Minh Y. Nguyen, Huynh Ly Tan Khoa, Suong Tieu  
**DOI:** [10.48084/etasr.17342](https://doi.org/10.48084/etasr.17342)  
**Article:** Engineering, Technology & Applied Science Research, Volume 16, Issue 2, pages 33925-33930, April 2026  
**Online:** 26 February 2026

WindFusion-EW is a hybrid ultra-short-term wind power forecasting framework. It combines CEEMDAN and Empirical Wavelet Transform for signal decomposition with a weighted ensemble of transformer-based forecasting models.
