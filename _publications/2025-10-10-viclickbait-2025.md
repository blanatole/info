**Authors:** Dai Phuoc Nguyen, Thien Khai Tran, Y Minh Nguyen, Bay Vo  
**DOI:** [10.1016/j.dib.2025.112164](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.dib.2025.112164)  
**Article:** Data in Brief, Volume 63, Article 112164, 2025  
**Dataset:** [Mendeley Data](https://data.mendeley.com/datasets/3wc46bfcjc/1)

ViClickbait-2025 is a curated Vietnamese-language dataset for automatic clickbait detection. It contains 3,414 headline samples collected from eight major Vietnamese online news platforms between 2023 and 2025, with annotations for clickbait and non-clickbait labels.
