**Authors:** Thanh-Long Nguyen, Phan Hoang Minh Phuoc, Huynh Ly Tan Khoa, Y. Nguyen Minh, Luu Van Nhat Hao  
**DOI:** [10.1007/978-3-032-21628-1_23](https://doi.org/10.1007/978-3-032-21628-1_23)  
**Book chapter:** Computational Intelligence in Engineering Science, ICCIES 2026, Communications in Computer and Information Science, Volume 2944, pages 318-328  
**First online:** 3 April 2026

This paper studies protein allergenicity prediction by integrating compositional sequence descriptors such as AAC, DPC, and CKSAAP with contextual embeddings from ProtBERT. Ensemble models are used to improve predictive stability and performance.
